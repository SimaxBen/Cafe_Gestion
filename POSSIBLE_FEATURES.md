# Possible Features to Add

Based on the current state of the Cafe Management System, here is a list of potential features that could be implemented to enhance the application:

## 1. Operations & Service
- **Table Management**: Visual layout of the cafe floor with tables. Assign orders to specific tables and track their status (Occupied, Reserved, Free).
- **Kitchen Display System (KDS)**: A dedicated view for kitchen staff to see incoming orders in real-time, mark them as "Preparing" or "Ready".
- **Receipt Printing**: Integration with thermal printers (USB/Bluetooth) to print receipts for customers and kitchen tickets.
- **Order Modifiers**: Add support for "Extra Sugar", "No Ice", "Takeaway" options on menu items.

## 2. Inventory & Supply Chain
- **Inventory Alerts**: Automatic notifications when stock items fall below a certain threshold.
- **Supplier Management**: Maintain a database of suppliers and link them to stock items.
- **Purchase Orders**: Generate and track purchase orders sent to suppliers.
- **Waste Tracking**: Log wasted or spoiled items to better calculate true costs.

## 3. Staff & HR
- **Shift Management**: Allow staff to Clock-in and Clock-out. Track working hours automatically.
- **User Roles & Permissions**: Granular permissions (e.g., Waiter can only create orders, Manager can see reports).
- **Performance Metrics**: Track sales by staff member to identify top performers.

## 4. Customer & Marketing
- **Loyalty Program**: Simple points system for customers (e.g., Buy 10 coffees, get 1 free).
- **Customer Database**: Save customer details and order history.
- **Discounts & Promotions**: Apply percentage or fixed amount discounts to orders.

## 5. Technical & UI/UX
- **Dark Mode**: Toggle between Light and Dark themes.
- **Multi-language Support**: Full support for switching between Arabic, English, and French.
- **Offline Mode**: Allow creating orders even when internet connection is lost (sync when back online).
- **Export Data**: Ability to export reports and lists to PDF or Excel/CSV.
- **Dashboard Widgets**: Customizable widgets on the home screen for quick stats.

## 6. Expansion
- **Online Ordering**: A public-facing page where customers can order ahead or for delivery.
- **Multi-branch Support**: Manage multiple cafe locations from a single admin account.
